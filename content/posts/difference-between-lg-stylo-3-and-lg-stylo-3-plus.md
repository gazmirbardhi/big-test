## Key differences between LG Stylo 3 and LG Stylo 3 Plus

### Design and Build

LG Stylo 3 has a plastic build with a sleek and stylish design, while LG Stylo 3 Plus has a metal build with a premium look and feel. Both smartphones come with a stylus that can be used for various tasks, such as taking notes, drawing, and more.

### Display

LG Stylo 3 comes with a 5.7-inch TFT display with a resolution of 720 x 1280 pixels, while LG Stylo 3 Plus has a 5.7-inch IPS LCD display with a resolution of 1080 x 1920 pixels. The display of LG Stylo 3 Plus is much clearer and sharper compared to LG Stylo 3.

### Processor and RAM

LG Stylo 3 is powered by a 1.4GHz Octa-Core Qualcomm Snapdragon 435 processor and has 2GB of RAM. On the other hand, LG Stylo 3 Plus is powered by a 1.4GHz Octa-Core Qualcomm Snapdragon 427 processor and has 2GB of RAM. Both smartphones offer a decent performance but LG Stylo 3 Plus offers slightly better performance than LG Stylo 3.

### Storage

LG Stylo 3 comes with 16GB of internal storage that can be expanded up to 2TB using a microSD card. LG Stylo 3 Plus has 32GB of internal storage that can be expanded up to 2TB using a microSD card. So, LG Stylo 3 Plus offers more storage compared to LG Stylo 3.

### Camera

LG Stylo 3 has a 13MP primary camera and a 5MP front-facing camera. On the other hand, LG Stylo 3 Plus has a 13MP primary camera and a 5MP front-facing camera. Both smartphones offer decent camera quality but there is no major difference between the two in terms of camera quality.

### Battery

LG Stylo 3 is powered by a 3080mAh battery, while LG Stylo 3 Plus has a larger 3000mAh battery. The battery life of both smartphones is decent but LG Stylo 3 Plus has a larger battery capacity compared to LG Stylo 3.

### Other features

Both LG Stylo 3 and LG Stylo 3 Plus run on Android 7.0 Nougat and come with various connectivity options such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS, and more. LG Stylo 3 Plus has a fingerprint sensor that provides an added layer of security, while LG Stylo 3 does not have a fingerprint sensor.
